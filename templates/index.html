    How to Play Word Hunt

    Rules:
    • Use the letters to create as many words as you can
    • Center letter (highlighted blue) must be in the word
    • Words must be at least 4 letters long
    • Letters can be used more than once
    Scoring:
    • 4 letter words = 1 point
    • Longer words are worth 1 point per letter
      (7 letter word = 7 points)
